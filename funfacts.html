Here are 3 birds from Ontario that I find interesting/beautiful/fascinating.

1. Red-Tailed Hawk

2. Mute Swan

3. Pileated Woodpecker
   
 

Red-Tailed Hawk

 

The classic "roar" of an eagle that you hear in most Hollywood Movies actually originates from the Red-Tailed Hawk's scream.

  
     
       
 

Mute Swan

 

Swans mate for life, meaning that once they find a partner they stick with that partner for the rest of their lives! However, change in mates can occur if their partner dies.

   		  
   
   
 

Pileated Woodpecker

 

The rectangular holes that the pileated woodpecker drills into trees are used so it can feed off the ants living inside the trunk. The holes can go so deep to the point they can even cause small trees to split in half!
